What is Population Collapse?

Population collapse refers to a rapid and significant decline in a country's or region's population. It is driven by a combination of low fertility rates, aging populations, and reduced immigration. As birth rates fall below replacement levels and life expectancy increases, many nations are beginning to experience more deaths than births, leading to long-term demographic and economic challenges.
